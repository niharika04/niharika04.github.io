About Me

I am Niharika (Saxena) Shravah. Having completed my bachelor's in the field of bio-medical engineering, I have always been fascinated about the medical machines and devices. Over a period of time I have inculcated my attentiveness in the spectrum of designing.

I have worked as a Bio-Medical Engineer with Healthcare industries like Stryker and Karl Storz and as a Product Designer with Cubical Labs, an IoT startup, in India.

I am interested in creating products that are used on a daily basis, which have a specific utility and helping to create better future for mankind. I am a creative person, who is eager to learn and who is fascinated by different fields of design, such as graphic design, web design, industrial design etc.
